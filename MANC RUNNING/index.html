WHY RUN WITH MANC RUNNING?

BE HEALTHY

Best way to improve your aerobic ability is to RUN! Nothing else more naturally and effectively improves it.

BE FREE

There is a tranquility and feeling of being ALIVE when running that other activities do not achieve. Endorphins released in the brain during running contribute to this feeling.

BE SOCIAL

Running with others can improve the enjoyment and inspire you to achieve greater performance.

BE STRONG

Running strengthens your heart, muscles and joints.
